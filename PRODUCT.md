# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primarily the owner — building a personal digital home that mixes travel and creative work. Secondary: anyone who discovers it (collaborators, employers, curious strangers). The site should feel personal enough to share with a close friend and polished enough to earn a stranger's attention.

## Product Purpose

Bijin Beyond Borders is a personal site mixing travel stories and creative work. Not a blog, not a conventional portfolio. A curated, beautiful record of a life lived across places and interests — a world worth visiting. "Bijin" (美人, Japanese for beautiful person) names the sensibility: looking carefully, moving with intention.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, intentional, wandering. The site should feel like it was made by someone who photographs things carefully and notices what others miss. Personal without being precious. Polished without being cold.

## Anti-references

- Generic travel blog templates with card grids and pastel palettes
- Cream-and-warm-neutral "AI aesthetic" backgrounds
- Corporate portfolio sites with hero metrics and stats panels
- Sites that read as a theme, not a person
- Editorial-magazine serif-italic aesthetics applied by reflex (editorial is one lane, not the default)

## Design Principles

1. The traveler, not the destination — center the person's perspective, not the places
2. Edit ruthlessly — fewer, better moments over completeness
3. Motion earns its place — animation reveals, it does not decorate
4. Typography is voice — font choice is a design decision, not a default
5. Specificity over category — every image and word should be singular, not representative

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion fully respected with equivalent non-animated fallbacks. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast ratio on all backgrounds. No motion-dependent content.
