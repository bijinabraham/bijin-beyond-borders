Who I am

I move through places
the way some people
move through books.

Completely. With attention to the margins. This site is where I keep what stays with me.

Not a travel blog. Not a portfolio. Something in between — a place where the work and the wandering live in the same room. You might find a photo from a city I've already forgotten, next to a project I'm still thinking about.

I'm interested in the specific. The exact color of afternoon light in a particular neighborhood. The way a certain material feels. The version of a city that only exists at a particular hour. This is where I keep those things.
